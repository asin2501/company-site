Stories told through gold & silver ink.

THE MURAL

When we moved into our space last year, it was important to make it our own. We gave the job to one of our designers, Mauri Vio, to create a fun mural that incorporates what makes us tick. With the help of several Jam3 designers, a tongue-in-cheek mural was created. At the center of it is a saying we use at Jam3–’Make It Happen & Don’t Fuck It Up’. It highlights our belief in ownership, or, as we like to call it, ‘own-your-shit.’ Whether you’re an artist or a tech wizard, when you’re proud of the work you do, it shows in the end results.

BREAKING STEREOTYPES

We’re proud of our Canadiana roots; and no, not everyone here has a pet beaver or a moose. The moose you see here is a symbol of our Canadian roots, but it’s also a pretty hip moose, he doesn’t adhere to ordinary moose stereotypes. In essence, Jam3 is very similar.

JAM3 SAUCE

‘Where’s the sauce?!’ Is something one of our Creative Directors, Pablo, always asks. The sauce is really what makes the meal memorable. And in our case, it’s the secret ingredient that takes our projects from great to award-winning. It’s that extra craft, the details and love that goes into our design and motion that makes the end-product truly special. You can also see two of our Creative Directors (and founding Partners) pixel-pushing a project; from day one they’ve been undyingly passionate about everything looking just right.

TRADITIONS

Like every great family, we place a lot of value on tradition. It’s what makes Jam3 so tight. Armageddon Wings Night is one of our longest standing rituals. It started when the company consisted of only the three Partners, at lunch time they’d head to a pub across the street and dare each other to try them, everyone always jumped in on the experience. As the company grew, the tradition remained, and is now a way to say ‘you’re now in the family.’ The wings are crazy spicy though, so most people can only handle one. Every now and then there is that one person who can eat over a dozen–that’s seriously impressive.

OUR ROOTS

Toronto, or ‘Tronno’ as some might say, is what we call home. It’s where it all started. And what’s more symbolic of this city than our giant - and quite phallic - CN Tower. We’ve moved offices several times - always bigger and better - and have seen Toronto grow into a dynamic hotbed for digital storytelling. Even though we’ve recently opened offices in Europe, South America, and Los Angeles, Toronto will always be at the heart of the company.
