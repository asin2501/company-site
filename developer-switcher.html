Design with intent

We’re passionate about our craft. That’s why this internship is a hands-on exploration of creativity and design.

We believe in learning by doing. Our aim is to nurture and grow bright young minds from the get-go, because that’s how our industry will thrive.

01

WHAT TO EXPECT

the role

At Jam3, you’ll be exposed to every discipline in our creative process – UX, UI, interactive design, motion design, and copywriting. You’ll be part of a dynamic team that’s made up of likeminded, talented people. With them, you’ll work hard to create something that will - without a doubt - stand out in your portfolio.

In addition to your team project, you’ll be lending a hand to the creative team. Participating in brainstorm sessions, building winning pitches, preparing assets and design bibles for productions, you’ll be in the thick of it all.

We’ll assign you a mentor who will be there to support you, teach you, and answer all of your questions.

Oh, and you’ll probably make a friend or ten. NBD

REQUIREMENTS

You own your shit. You’re a self-starter who shows passion and dedication to every task given. "Make it happen and give all the fucks", is what we like to say–and the attitude we need to see from you. Here is a straightforward breakdown:

  • You play nice with other people.
  • You're an actively engaged person. That means you are present, motivated, and social.
  • You excel at photoshop. You can create initial design layouts with confidence and know how to effectively communicate concepts to everyone on your team.
  • You have a solid understanding of visual hierarchy and colour theory.
  • You will assist with miscellaneous front-end build production if needed (slicing, layout, etc.).
  • You have creative courage. This is a tough industry, only the brave and sturdy remain. Be prepared to defend your decisions and take constructive criticism.

Here’s a little peek of how your day could play out:
  • 9:30 - Coffee and talking about last night’s Netflix binge.
  • 10:00 - Scrum with the team.
  • 12:00 - Eating lunch with the team. Laughter and debates ensue.

CODE LIKE YOU MEAN IT

We love to make things. Really awesome things. That’s why we created an internship that empowers you to code the impossible into possible.

We believe in learning by doing. Our aim is to nurture and grow bright young minds from the get-go, because that’s how our industry will thrive.

01

WHAT TO EXPECT

the role

We’re looking for creative coders. At Jam3, our developers, creative technologists, and technical directors are special because they can think both logistically and artistically. We value skill and aptitude, but we honour those who care about working with their team to add the ‘Jam3 sauce’ to every project.

You’ll be part of a dynamic team that’s made up of likeminded, talented people. With them, you’ll work hard to create something that will - without a doubt - stand out in your portfolio. You’ll produce standards based code, surface risk, and ask great questions, while fostering a solid relationship with your teammates.

You are someone who people can count on. Every day, you come in ready to make great things happen.
